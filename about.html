About Us

Elevate Your Performance. Transform Your Life.

Our Story

Urban Performance Club was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves access to world-class training, expert coaching, and a supportive community.

What started as a small warehouse gym in 2018 has grown into a premier performance facility featuring state-of-the-art strength training equipment and a dedicated boxing gym. We serve athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and combat sports practitioners looking to push their limits. Our approach combines cutting-edge training methodologies with personalized attention, creating an environment where everyone—from beginners to elite athletes—can achieve their goals.

We're not just a gym. We're a community of driven individuals who believe in the power of consistent effort, mental toughness, and supporting each other through every challenge. Whether you're training for competition, rebuilding after injury, or simply want to feel stronger and more confident, Urban Performance Club is your partner in progress.

Our Facility

Strength Zone

Olympic lifting platforms, competition-grade barbells, power racks, and a complete range of free weights for serious strength development.

Conditioning Area

Assault bikes, rowing machines, ski ergs, battle ropes, and dedicated space for high-intensity interval training and metabolic conditioning.

Boxing Gym

Professional boxing ring, heavy bags, speed bags, double-end bags, and full equipment for technical training and sparring sessions.

Group Training Space

Dedicated area for small group sessions and partner training with full equipment access and coach supervision.

Private Training Rooms

Two soundproof rooms for personal training sessions with dedicated equipment and individualized attention.

Amenities

Clean locker rooms, hot showers, secure storage, filtered water stations, and a members lounge with WiFi.
